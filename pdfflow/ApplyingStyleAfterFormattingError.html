ApplyingStyleAfterFormattingError

Exception type:
InvalidOperationException

May occur:
When applying a style to a content element.

Cause:
The library does not allow applying a style to a content element after changing its formatting. If you need both to apply a style and configure the formatting, apply the style first and then change the formatting. For more on styles and formatting, see the article Formatting and Styles.

Method(s):
LineBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), ParagraphBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), ImageBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), InlineImageBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), TableCellBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), TableBuilder.ApplyStyle(StyleBuilder), TableBuilder.SetStyle(StyleBuilder)
