InvalidPageSize

Exception type:
ArgumentException InvalidSizeException

May occur:

  1. When trying to get a page size using a value from the PaperSize enumeration.

  2. When working with a document flow area by coordinates.

Cause:

  1. The PredefinedSizeBuilder.ToSize(PaperSize) method cannot determine the page size of the passed PaperSize value.

  2. The even or the odd document flow area has a negative value, or the page width or height has a negative value.

Method(s):

  1. PredefinedSizeBuilder.ToSize(PaperSize)

  2. DocumentBuilder.Build(string), DocumentBuilder.Build(Stream), DocumentBuilder.AddSection, DocumentBuilder.AddSectionToDocument
