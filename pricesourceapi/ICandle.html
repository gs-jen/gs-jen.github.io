class ICandle

Brief

The class represents a candle

Details

A candle is the way to aggregate all the prices during a period of time into four key prices:

The open price is the first price during the period of the time.

The high price is the maximum price reached during the period of time.

The low price is the minimum price reached during the period of time.

The close price is the last price during the period of time.

The name comes from good old visual technique used to analyze stock prices called japanese candlestick charts wiki

Public Methods

getClose

Returns the close price of the candle.

getHigh

Returns the high price of the candle.

getLow

Returns the low price of the candle.

getOpen

Returns the open price of the candle.

getStart

Returns the date and time when the candle started.

getVolume

Returns the volume of the candle.
