class PriceArchiveClient.Candle

Brief

A candle

Details

A candle is the way to aggregate all prices for a period of time into four key prices:

  • Open price - the first price during the period of the time.

  • High price - the maximum price reached during the period of time.

  • Low price - the minimum price reached during the period of time.

  • Close price - the last price during the period of time.

The name comes from good old visual technique used to analyze stock prices called Japanese candlestick charts (see Wiki).

Public Methods

Gets the close price of the candle.

getHigh

Gets the high price of the candle.

getLow

Gets the low price of the candle.

getOpen

Gets the open price of the candle.

getVolume

Gets the volume of the candle.
